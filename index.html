Dimension 1
Business Question & Purpose
Does every page serve a clear question? Does critical info appear first for this audience?
Dimension 2
Domain Knowledge
Are these the right metrics for this industry? Does it treat a crisis as a crisis?
Dimension 3
Audience Fit & Interactivity
Is the depth, detail, and interactivity right for who will actually use this?
Dimension 4
Data Accuracy & Story
Facts vs insights test. Internal consistency. Time horizon adequacy.
Dimension 5
Visual Design & Consistency
Right chart types? Anti-patterns flagged. Top-left priority. Accessibility.
Dimension 6
Actionability & The So What
Every pattern → consequence → action. Calibrated to this audience's decision authority.
